Bena Association for Community Care

  • vision

    The first and distinguished association in providing charitable and developmental services to marginalized groups in Palestine.

  • mission

    Bena Association is a non-profit charitable association that seeks to provide exceptional integrated services to the target groups through a package of innovative projects committed to the principles of human rights and transparency in the provision of services.

Association achievements

  • Providing in-kind assistance to marginalized and needy groups in Palestinian society, especially orphans, poor families, and those with no income

  • Providing psychological and social support to marginalized groups through periodic home visits.

  • Providing a detailed database of poor families and orphan families

  • Medical days benefited large numbers of poor and needy families
